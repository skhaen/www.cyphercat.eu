Films et arts martiaux

Après le post sur les mangas et les arts martiaux, voici une courte liste de bons films d’art martiaux (selon mes goûts, bien entendu), et non seulement de baston. J’ai mis pour la plupart les liens vers IMDB, je pense que vous êtes capables de les (re)trouver sur vos trackers préférés.

Pour le moment, vous trouverez ci-dessous des films concernant :

  • Aikido
  • Karate
  • Kenjutsu (Iaido, Kendo, …)
  • Kung-fu
  • Misc (un mélange des genres, souvent plus des blockbusters)
  • Muay Thai
  • Pencat-Silat
  • Tai-chi

Je sais, on peut en rajouter encore beaucoup, et je le ferai au fur et à mesure, mais n’hésitez pas à m’envoyer un mail si vous avez des suggestions.

Je sais aussi qu’il manque au minimum un film de Jackie Chan, mais je ne me suis toujours pas décidé pour lequel.

AIKIDO

Street story (2014)

Czech short movie - 04m20

KARATE

Kuro-obi (aka “Black Belt”, 2007)

♥♥♥♥♥

The events take place in 1932 in Japanese-occupied Manchuria, in which the corrupt leaders of the Japanese army are trying to take over all the Karate dojos for their own personal benefit. Amidst these circumstances the master of one of these dojos dies before passing on the Kuroobi to his successor, leaving three of his pupils the task of deciding amongst themselves who deserves it most. After they bury their master, they are forced to leave the dojo and join the Japanese army. At this point, their journey leads them on rather different paths both in life and in the understanding of their master's teachings of martial arts

With Tatsuya Naka (Taikan) 7th Dan JKA Shotokan karate, Akihito Yagi (Giryu) 7th Dan Meibukan Gōjū-ryū Karate) and Yuji Suzuki (Choei) 1st Dan Kyokushin karate.

KENJUTSU

4th Dan

A short fiction film about a kendo student preparing for his exam.
  • part 1 (8m50),
  • part 2 (5m06),
  • part 3 (6m55).

Ame agaru (aka After the rain, Après la pluie - 1999)

During a downpour, a generous ronin and his supporting wife are stranded at a country inn. The ronin comes to the attention of a lord who wants to hire him as an instructor for his men, who treat the ronin with disrespect.

## Shichinin no samurai (aka Seven Samurais, Les sept samouraïs - 1954)

A poor village under attack by bandits recruits seven unemployed samurai to help them defend themselves.

By Akira Kurosawa, with Toshirô Mifune <3

KUNG-FU

Huo yuanjia (aka Fearless, Le maître d’armes - 2006)

Martial arts legend Huo Yuanjia became the most famous fighter in all of China at the turn of the 20th Century. Huo faced personal tragedy but ultimately fought his way out of darkness and into history.

With Jet Li.

Kung fu (aka Crazy kung-fu - 2004)

In Shanghai, China in the 1940s, a wannabe gangster aspires to join the notorious "Axe Gang" while residents of a housing complex exhibit extraordinary powers in defending their turf.

Action and comedy movie!

Yip Man (aka Ip man - 2008)

During the Japanese invasion of 1937 a wealthy martial artist if forced to leave his home and work to support his family, he reluctantly agrees to train others in Wing Chun for self-defense.

With Donnie Yen

MISC

John Wick (2014)

An ex-hitman comes out of retirement to track down the gangsters that took everything from him.

With Keanu Reeves

Kiss of the Dragon (aka Le baiser mortel du dragon - 2001)

A betrayed intelligence officer enlists the aid of a prostitute to prove his innocence from a deadly conspiracy while returning a favor to her.

With Jet Li.

##  MUAY THAI

Ong-bak (2003)

When the head of a statue sacred to a village is stolen, a young martial artist goes to the big city and finds himself taking on the underworld to retrieve it.

With Tony Jaa.

PENCAT-SILAT

Serbuan maut (The Raid - 2011)

A S.W.A.T. team becomes trapped in a tenement run by a ruthless mobster and his army of killers and thugs.

With Iko Uwais (Silat) and Joe Taslim (Judo)

TAI-CHI

Man of Tai Chi (2013)

A young martial artist’s unparalleled Tai Chi skills land him in a highly lucrative underworld fight club.
With Tiger Hu Chen and Keanu Reeves
